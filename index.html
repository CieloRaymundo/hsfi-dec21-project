Marc's scriptEd Summary Project!

Main Goals

  1. In the section that appears when you press "ABOUT ME", write your name and include:
    - a list of your top 3 favorite foods in order from 1 to 3
    - a list of some of your favorite musicians, in no particular order
    - a picture of your favorite food
    - a picture of one of your favorite musicians
  2. Use one or more custom google fonts on your site.
  3. Fix your site so that as soon as it loads, the PROJECT GOALS section is displayed (without needing to click on it first!).
  4. Creation a section for MY PAST PROJECTS that displays a list of links to some of the past projects you've worked on in class, including your Simpsons project and your Halloween Project
  5. When you click on the DARK THEME button, change the background color of your site and the text color so it has a dark background and the font is still readable.
  6. When you click on the DO NOT PRESS button, show a pop-up over your site that says "You shouldn't have pressed that!"
  7. When you click on the TAKEOVER MY SITE button, show a form input and a prompt that asks for your name. When you enter your name, change the title of the site to "This site now belongs to: [name that was inputted]"

Bonus Goals

  1. Place 5 of the icons available in Twitter Bootstrop here on this site.
  2. Learn more about CSS by playing the Flexbox Froggy game
